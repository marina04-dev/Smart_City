Sustainability News & Updates

Global Shift to Renewables.

Recent reports indicate that solar and wind energy are now cheaper than coal in most major economies. Cities are adopting "Smart Grids" to manage this power more efficiently.

The Rise of the Circular Economy.

Major brands are moving away from "take-make-waste" models. New legislation pushes for "Right to Repair," ensuring electronics last longer and stay out of landfills.
