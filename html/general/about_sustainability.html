What is it all about?

The Core Definition

"Sustainability means meeting our own needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs."

It is often described as having three pillars: Environmental (Planet), Social (People), and Economic (Profit). A truly sustainable solution must balance all three.

Why It Matters

From reducing greenhouse gas emissions to preserving fresh water, sustainable living ensures that our planet remains habitable and healthy.

Our Mission

SmartCity aims to bridge the gap between complex climate science and actionable daily habits. We believe that small individual changes—like composting, walking more, or fixing a leaky tap—collectively lead to massive global impact.
