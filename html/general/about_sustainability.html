What is Sustainability?

Sustainability means meeting our own needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It is not just about the environment; it is also about social equity and economic vitality.

Why It Matters

From reducing greenhouse gas emissions to preserving fresh water, sustainable living ensures that our planet remains habitable and healthy. SmartCity aims to bridge the gap between complex science and actionable daily habits.
