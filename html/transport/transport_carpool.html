Fewer Cars, Cleaner Air

The average car carries just 1.5 people. If everyone shared a ride, we could cut traffic in half. Carpooling is the easiest way to reduce your carbon footprint without giving up the convenience of a car.

Why Carpool?

  • Cost Savings: Split the cost of gas, tolls, and parking with your passengers.
  • Preferred Parking: Many companies and universities offer reserved spots for carpoolers.
  • HOV Lanes: Use High Occupancy Vehicle lanes to bypass traffic jams.

Technology Makes It Easy

You don't need to organize it manually. Apps like BlaBlaCar, Waze Carpool, and corporate solutions match you with neighbors going the same way.

Social Benefits

Carpooling turns a lonely, stressful commute into a social event. It builds community connections and reduces road rage by sharing the driving responsibility.
