The Foundation of a Smart City

Walking is the most sustainable, equitable, and healthy form of transport. A truly "Smart City" is designed for people, not cars. The modern urban planning concept of the "15-Minute City" aims to put all daily needs (shops, schools, parks) within a short walk of your home.

Why Walk?

  • Physical Health: Just 30 minutes of walking daily reduces heart disease risk by 19% and strengthens bones.
  • Mental Clarity: Walking reduces cortisol (stress hormone) levels and boosts creative thinking.
  • Zero Emissions: It is the only mode of transport that is completely carbon-free.

Safety Tips for Urban Walkers

Stay safe while navigating the concrete jungle:

  1. Be Seen: Wear reflective gear or light colors at night.
  2. Unplug: Keep volume low on headphones so you can hear traffic.
  3. Eye Contact: Make sure drivers see you before crossing a street.
