Two Wheels, Zero Emissions

Bicycles are the most energy-efficient vehicle ever invented. In congested cities, bikes are often faster than cars for distances under 5km. Cycling infrastructure, like protected bike lanes, is essential for a safe smart city.

The E-Bike Revolution

Electric bikes (E-bikes) are a game-changer. They flatten hills and shorten distances, allowing people to commute without arriving at work sweaty. They replace car trips more effectively than regular bikes.

Essential Gear Checklist

  • Helmet: Non-negotiable for safety.
  • Lights: White front light and red rear light for visibility.
  • U-Lock: Invest in a solid lock to prevent theft.
  • Panniers/Basket: Let the bike carry the weight, not your back.

Safety First: Taking the Lane

Don't hug the curb where debris gathers. It is safer to "take the lane" (ride in the center) when the road is too narrow for a car to pass you safely. This makes you more visible to drivers.
