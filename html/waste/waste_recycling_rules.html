Not all plastic is recyclable. Putting the wrong item in the bin can contaminate an entire truckload. When in doubt, throw it out.

♻️ Can I Recycle This?

Enter the plastic code number (found in the triangle) to check.

Know Your Codes

  • #1 (PET) & #2 (HDPE): Bottles and jugs. Highly recyclable.
  • #6 (PS): Styrofoam. Generally NOT recyclable in curbside bins.
