No Garden? No Problem.

You don't need a huge backyard to grow your own food. Urban farming utilizes balconies, windowsills, and rooftops to grow fresh produce. It reduces food miles to zero and greens our concrete cities.

Best Plants for Beginners

Start small with plants that thrive in pots and are hard to kill:

  • Herbs: Basil, Mint, and Rosemary love sunny windowsills.
  • Leafy Greens: Lettuce and Spinach grow fast and don't need deep soil.
  • Cherry Tomatoes: They produce abundant fruit in a single large pot.
  • Chili Peppers: Perfect for hot, sunny balconies.

Container Gardening Tips

  1. Drainage: Ensure your pots have holes at the bottom so roots don't rot.
  2. Sunlight: Most vegetables need at least 6 hours of direct sun.
  3. Watering: Pots dry out faster than the ground. Check them daily in summer!
