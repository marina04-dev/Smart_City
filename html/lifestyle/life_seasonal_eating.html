  • About Sustainability

Seasonal eating means consuming food harvested at the time you buy it. It reduces the energy needed for greenhouses and shipping.

What to Eat When

  • Spring: Asparagus, Spinach, Peas.
  • Summer: Tomatoes, Corn, Berries.
  • Autumn: Pumpkins, Apples, Squash.
  • Winter: Root vegetables, Citrus, Kale.
