Nature's Schedule

Seasonal eating means consuming food that is harvested at the same time of year you buy it. In our modern world, we can buy strawberries in December, but they likely flew thousands of miles to reach us. Eating seasonally reconnects us with nature's cycles.

Why It Matters

  • Better Taste: Food ripened on the vine tastes sweeter and richer than food ripened in a truck.
  • Higher Nutrition: Vitamins degrade over time. Fresh, local produce retains more nutrients.
  • Lower Carbon Footprint: Avoiding air-freighted food reduces "food miles" significantly.
