  • About Sustainability

Solar Photovoltaic (PV) panels convert sunlight directly into electricity. By installing panels on your roof, you can generate your own clean power, reducing reliance on the grid.

Why Go Solar?

  • Financial Freedom: Lower your monthly bills and sell excess power back to the grid.
  • Grid Independence: With battery storage, your home remains powered during blackouts.
  • Eco-Friendly: A typical residential system offsets 3-4 tons of carbon annually.
