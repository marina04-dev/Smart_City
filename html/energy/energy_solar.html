What is Solar Power?

Solar power is energy generated from the suns light and heat. Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into electricity that can be used to power homes, schools, and businesses.

This electricity is produced using photovoltaic (PV) cells, which create an electric current when exposed to sunlight. Solar power is clean, renewable, and produces no harmful emissions.

Why would someone choose Solar Power?

Solar power is a clean, renewable energy source that helps reduce electricity costs while protecting the environment. By using energy from the sun, homes and businesses can become more sustainable and energy-independent.

Enviromental Benefits

  • Its a renewable energy source
  • It helps slow down climate change
  • Protects natural resources

Financial Benefits

  • Lower monthly electricity bills
  • Free energy once panels are installed(Not including maintanence costs)
  • Protection from rising energy prices

Practical advantages

  • Reduced reliance on the electricity grid
  • Greter control over energy usage
  • Ideal for off-grid locations

Is solar right for you?

Solar Photovoltaic (PV) panels convert sunlight directly into electricity. By installing panels on your roof, you can generate your own clean power, reducing reliance on the grid.
