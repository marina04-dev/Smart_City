Just 1 inch of rain on a roof can yield over 600 gallons of water. Harvesting this water is free and keeps your garden green during droughts.

System Components

  • Catchment: Your roof surface.
  • Storage: Rain barrels or cisterns.
  • Diverter: A "first-flush" system to remove debris.
