The Easy Upgrade

Installing low-flow fixtures is one of the most effective ways to save water at home. Modern aerators reduce water usage by 30-50% without sacrificing pressure by mixing air into the stream.

Top Upgrades for Your Home

  • Dual-Flush Toilets: Allow you to choose a small flush (3L) for liquids and a larger flush (6L) for solids, saving thousands of liters a year.
  • Low-Flow Showerheads: Standard heads use 15+ liters per minute. Efficient ones use less than 8 liters.
  • Tap Aerators: A cheap attachment that screws onto your faucet to reduce flow while maintaining pressure.
